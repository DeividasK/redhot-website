The idea

We think that knowledge is power...

...and the power must be shared.

We noticed that our customers often judge the quality of the pellets by their color. We, being curious creatures, decided to check if this is true.

After learning all the ins and outs of pellet production the old adage trumped again - you can't judge a book by its cover. And that was also true with ENplus certified pellets.

Even the best pellets can be dark (different wood species).

So we set on a quest to share this information with others...
